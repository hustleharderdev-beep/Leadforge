FAQ

Everything You
Want to Know.

How quickly will I see results?
+
Most clients start seeing qualified leads in the first 2–4 weeks. A fully optimized pipeline with consistent results typically takes 60–90 days. We're transparent about timelines from day one.
Do I need a big existing audience?
+
Not at all. Leadforge is built for businesses that want to create demand from scratch — not amplify existing audiences. Cold outreach and systematic pipeline building work regardless of your current following.
What industries do you work with?
+
We primarily work with B2B businesses — SaaS, consulting, agencies, professional services, real estate, and manufacturing. If you sell to other businesses, we can almost certainly help you.
Is there a minimum contract length?
+
We ask for a 3-month minimum commitment — not to lock you in, but because sustainable lead generation systems take time to build and optimize. Most clients stay 12+ months because the results compound.
How is Leadforge different from agencies?
+
Most agencies charge ₹1L+/month for generic playbooks. We build custom systems tailored to your exact ICP, market, and sales motion — and we're obsessively focused on measurable revenue outcomes, not vanity metrics.
Will you handle execution or just strategy?
+
Both. Depending on your plan, we handle strategy, copywriting, CRM setup, outreach execution, and reporting. The Forge and Inferno plans are truly done-for-you — you just show up for the calls.
What tools do you use?
+
We work with HubSpot, Apollo, Instantly, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Clay, and custom AI workflows. We adapt to your existing tech stack wherever possible to minimize disruption.
Do you offer a guarantee?
+
Yes. If you don't see a meaningful improvement in qualified lead volume within 90 days on the Forge or Inferno plan, we'll work for free the following month until we hit your targets. We're that confident.
